About LaunchPad 🚀

Our Vision & Mission

Connecting students with real internship opportunities and helping them gain practical experience.

💡 Vision

Bridging the gap between universities and companies through a smart internship platform.

🚀 Goal

Preparing students for the job market and supporting their professional growth.
